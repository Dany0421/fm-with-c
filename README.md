# ⚽ FOOTBALL MANAGER — Vibe Coded

A fully playable, browser-based Football Manager game built with **vanilla JavaScript, HTML and CSS**. No frameworks, no back-end, no database – everything runs client-side.

🔗 **Live demo**: https://dany0421.github.io/fm-with-c/

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏆 League & Competition Management

- **Multi-league system** – Premier League, LaLiga, Serie A, Bundesliga, Liga Portugal (with promotion/relegation)
- **Domestic Cups** – FA Cup, Copa del Rey, Coppa Italia, DFB-Pokal, Taça de Portugal
- **European Competitions** – Champions League & Europa League (realistic qualification based on league positions)
- **Dynamic league tables** – Auto-updating standings, fixtures, and results
- **Season history** – Complete records of all competitions

### 🎮 Advanced Match Engine

- **Minute-by-minute simulation** – Live match commentary and events
- **Advanced statistics**:
  - xG (Expected Goals)
  - Possession %
  - Shots on/off target
  - Big chances created/missed
  - Pass accuracy
  - Momentum tracking
- **Match events**:
  - Goals (headers, penalties, free kicks, long shots)
  - Cards (yellow, red, 2nd yellow)
  - Injuries during matches
  - Substitutions (manual & automatic)
  - Penalties & direct free kicks
- **Player ratings** – Individual performance scores (1-10) after each match
- **Tactical impact** – Your formation, mentality, pressing & tempo affect results

### 👥 Complete Staff System

- **Assistant Manager** – Improves team morale and provides tactical advice
- **Fitness Coach** – Enhances player fitness recovery and conditioning
- **Physio** – Reduces injury risk and speeds up recovery
- **Set Piece Coach** – Improves penalty/free kick conversion rates
- **Staff levels** – Upgrade your staff for better bonuses

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Choose your team** – Start a new career and select a club from any league
2. **Manage your squad** – Pick your starting XI, set tactics, browse the transfer market
3. **Upgrade facilities** – Invest in stadium, training ground, and youth academy
4. **Hire staff** – Assistant manager, fitness coach, physio, set piece coach
5. **Train players** – Individual programs to improve specific attributes
6. **Run marketing campaigns** – Boost reputation and income
7. **Simulate matches** – Watch the engine calculate results based on stats, form, morale, and traits
8. **Follow the season** – League tables, cup draws, European qualification – all update automatically
9. **Negotiate sponsorships** – Secure deals to increase your budget
10. **Build a legacy** – Win titles, develop youth, climb the Hall of Fame

## 🐛 Known Limitations

- No multiplayer/sync across devices

- Best experienced on desktop (mobile optimized but smaller screen)
